Common Student Mistakes

"Smart people learn from their mistakes. Wise people learn from others' mistakes."

1

The Friend Factor

Taking Science just because your best friend took it. They might be good at it; you might not be.

2

Sharma Ji's Son

Following a career because society says it's "respectable" while ignoring your own interest.

3

Ignoring Skills

Focusing only on degrees and marks while ignoring real-world skills like communication and tech.

4

FOMO Trends

Jumping into "Stock Trading" or "Crypto" or "Influencing" just because it looks cool on Instagram.
